Our Services

Experience comprehensive logistics solutions tailored to your unique needs. Our team of experts is dedicated to providing efficient and reliable transportation services.

Air Transport

Our air freight services ensure your shipments are delivered swiftly and securely to destinations worldwide. We work with a network of trusted airlines to offer competitive rates and reliable transit times.

Cargo Transport

We specialize in handling both general and specialized cargo, ensuring safe and efficient transportation. Our team is equipped to manage customs procedures and documentation, ensuring your shipments comply with all regulations.

Trucks Transport

Our fleet of modern trucks allows us to provide efficient and reliable road transportation services within Uganda and across borders. We offer flexible options to suit your specific needs, including full truck load (FTL) and partial truck load (PTL) services.

Train Transport

For long-distance transportation within Uganda, we leverage the country's rail network to offer cost-effective and efficient solutions. Our team coordinates rail shipments to ensure timely delivery and minimize transit times.

About Us

Laiken Logistics established in 2010 has since been at the forefront of Uganda's logistics industry, delivering marine and air freight logistics services to business that import and export goods throughout East Africa. With a fleet of trucks at our disposal, cross boarder transportation of goods has never been easier.

In order to guarantee customer satisfaction, Laiken Logistics partners with cargo giant Bin Thani Express Cargo [BINTEC] in Dubai and China ensuring that our consignments are handled with care, tracked and monitored.

Whether you're an everyday importer or trying it for your first time, custom clearance and cross border logistics can be a nightmare! With our URA and KRA certified agents who are EAC compliant, we make the best out of the trade and you can forget about any nightmares when you have Laiken Logistics in your corner.
